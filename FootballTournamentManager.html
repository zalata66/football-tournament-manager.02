Football Tournament Manager

Welcome

Football Tournament Manager helps you create and run a complete football tournament from team setup to final awards.

You can add teams manually, build randomized teams from a player list, track match results and player statistics, use timers and tie-breaks, and finish with a full champion and awards summary.

Features

  • Round Robin, Double Round Robin, Knockout, Two-Legged Knockout, and Double Elimination.
  • Players, captains, goalkeepers, optional tiers, and optional positions.
  • Randomize and Level Randomize team builders.
  • Match timer, tie-breaks, brackets, standings, and final awards.
  • Top scorer, top assister, best keeper, best defender, and POTM.
  • Light mode, dark mode, export, and import.
